H2 shared-server extension validation
=====================================

H1 preservation
---------------
- No H1 simulator, policy, channel, configuration, metric, or committed
  results/full file was modified.
- N=1 P1/I1 and P2/I2 arrays are compared bitwise in the automated suite.

Performance gate and Pareto pruning
-----------------------------------
- Initial unpruned smoke projection: 6.08 h (> 4 h).
- Meet-option Pareto pruning was therefore enabled under the preregistered
  performance rule; the violation option is never pruned against meet actions.
- Pruned and unpruned I1/I2/J1/J2 violation, action, energy, and allocation
  arrays match exactly in the automated regression test.
- Final projected/full-equivalent time: smoke 3.027 h, quick 2.875 h,
  full measured projection 3.810 h.

-----------------------------
- H2a: reject on observed region; maximum high-load interaction =
  -1.603202 percentage points.

  maximum violation-spread increase 0.115874 and minimum Jain-index change
